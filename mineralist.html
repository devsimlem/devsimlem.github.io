The Mineralist

A Fast Paced Card Game

An educational card game based around combining elements together in order to form minerals! A competitive game where players must create minerals from a pool of elements! Be the first to reach the point limit and claim your title as a Mineralist!

This 10 week project included brainstorming a potential game, storyboarding the game, conceptualizing art, designing cards and the rulebook, and printing the cards on stock for production.

Skills developed: Brainstorming, storyboarding, designing cards, game balancing and tweaking, playtesting and playtest reporting, writing up game documentation (rulebook).
