Delivery Frenzy

A Couch Party Game

Grab your friends and find a couch because this couch party game will surely become your new favorite game! Play in teams of two and compete to collect and deliver food orders through 100s of exciting levels! But beware, collaboration is vital! One member of your team controls the left and right, while the other controls forward and reverse movements. This game is set to be released on Steam and AppleTV in the coming years!

This project is being developed with a few friends of mine, which has given me the chance to delve into areas I have never explored. While I have been programming a large part of the game, I have also stepped forward as the main artist, for both 2D and 3D art! I have never done any sort of 3D modelling before, so this project has been an incredible opportunity to learn a new skill!

Skills Developed: Unity, Git, Jira, Photoshop, Illustrator, Maya, Blender, Level Design.
